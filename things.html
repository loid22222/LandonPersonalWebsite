Simple Is Beautiful

Simple Is Beautiful is a farming simulator game where the only way to bring light to an otherwise gray, grim, and hopeless world is to bring new, vibrant plants to the world. This game is currently in its very early stages and is essentially just a demo of what the mechanics and art would be like later.
